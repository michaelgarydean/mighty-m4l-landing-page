Hardware Step Sequencer

A step sequencer with motorized faders, integrated with the Behringer BCF2000 MIDI controller. Features like “Fader Link” move all faders simultaneously by the exact same amount, or the per-step delay sequencer. Chaser lights offer real-time feedback on the controller so the performer never has to look at the computer.

Lots of funky patterns that you would not be able to do otherwise. Dreams come true.
